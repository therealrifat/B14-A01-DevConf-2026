Frequently Asked Questions

Will the sessions be recorded for post-event viewing?

Yes! All keynote speeches, technical tracks, and panel debates will be recorded and made available to all ticket tiers within 48 hours after the closing ceremony.

Can I upgrade my Virtual Ticket to an In-Person Pass later?

As long as the venue capacity has not been met, you can upgrade your ticket via your dashboard profile link sent to your registration email.

Are there team discounts available for corporate bookings?

Absolutely. We offer groups of 5 or more a 15% discount, and groups of 10 or more a 25% discount. Contact our corporate relations desk directly.
